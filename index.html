About me

I am a Design Verification Intern with a strong foundation in VLSI and digital design. Currently, I am working at IIT Hyderabad on a 5G testbed project, where I am gaining hands-on experience in RTL verification and protocol-level validation.

My expertise includes Verilog and SystemVerilog-based testbench development, functional verification using UVM, and validating digital designs through assertions, coverage, and simulation-based methodologies. I am passionate about understanding silicon at the nanoscale and ensuring design correctness before tape-out.
